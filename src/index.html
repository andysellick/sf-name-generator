The Game Name Generator

Unless you're a massive Street Fighter fan, you might not be aware that there have been literally hundreds of games bearing the Street Fighter name or featuring the game's characters. Amazingly, even though the first game was released in 1987 (Street Fighter), the most recent version is only Street Fighter V. How have so many Street Fighter games been released in nearly thirty years without increasing the series number beyond five? By prefixing and suffixing extra words, like 'super', 'turbo', and 'gold', often to extreme lengths, that's how.

In order to assist with this glacial pace of numerical incrementation, we humbly present the unofficial Street Fighter Game Name Generator. Simply click the generate button to see a suggested name. If you're lucky enough, you might even generate an existing, actual game name.
